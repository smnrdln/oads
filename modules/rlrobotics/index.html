📚 Course Outline

This trainer follows a four-level path from RL math and tabular methods to deep RL on robots.

  • Level 1: MDPs, Bellman backups, TD, Q-learning, robot state and action spaces
  • Level 2: Neural FA, DQN, policy gradients, actor-critic, PPO, sim, rewards, imitation
  • Level 3: Model-based RL, sim-to-real, SAC, TD3, HER, safety, locomotion, dexterity
  • Level 4: Offline RL, goal-conditioned control, multi-task/meta, foundation models, capstone
