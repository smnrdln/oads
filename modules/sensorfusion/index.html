📚 Course Outline

This trainer covers a four-level learning path with lessons, exercises, and scenarios.

  • Level 1: Beginner — signals, sampling, noise, and basic filtering
  • Level 2: Intermediate — spectra, digital filters, and Kalman filtering
  • Level 3: Advanced — frames, EKF/UKF, and fusion architectures
  • Level 4: Expert — diagnostics, INS/GNSS, capstone, and optional depth topics

Welcome to the Sensor Fusion & Signal Processing Trainer
