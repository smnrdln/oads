📚 Course Outline

This trainer follows a four-stage TinyML and Edge AI path from constraints to capstone design.

  • Level 1 (Beginner): constraints, hardware, ML basics, sensors, workflow
  • Level 2 (Intermediate): quantization, pruning, distillation, NAS, audio, vision, time series, profiling
  • Level 3 (Advanced): detection, on-device learning, co-design, MLOps, privacy
  • Level 4 (Expert): transformers, federated learning, system integration, capstone
