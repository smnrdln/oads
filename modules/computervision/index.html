📚 Course Outline

This trainer covers a four-level path from pixels to production vision systems.

  • Level 1: Images, classical CV, neural basics, CNNs, training metrics
  • Level 2: ResNets, transfer learning, classification, detection, segmentation
  • Level 3: ViTs, self-supervision, video, 3D, diffusion, NeRF, efficient models
  • Level 4: VLMs, editing, robustness, deployment, capstone design
