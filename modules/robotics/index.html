📚 Course Outline

This trainer covers a four-level learning path with lessons, exercises, and scenarios.

  • Level 1: Foundations (hardware, math, sensing, frames)
  • Level 2: Kinematics, dynamics, control, mobile bases
  • Level 3: Estimation, SLAM, perception, planning, manipulation
  • Level 4: Learning, fleets, safety, full-system design
