Privacy Policy

This page describes how Open Academy Dot Space and the Electronics and PCB Design Trainer handle data. These sites are static websites. They do not provide user accounts or a backend application for learner data.

Who this policy applies to

This policy applies to the Open Academy Dot Space site at openacademy.space and the Electronics and PCB Design Trainer available through the PCB module.

What the sites store locally

The PCB learning app stores some information in your browser so the trainer can remember your progress and preferences on your device.

  • learning progress, XP, completed topics, and achievements
  • language selection
  • theme preference

This information is stored locally in your browser and is not submitted to a custom application server operated by this project.

Advertising

Open Academy Dot Space may use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies, local storage, or similar technologies to serve and measure ads. You can learn more in Google’s own privacy materials and ad settings pages.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Ad Settings

Server logs and hosting

Like most static websites, the hosting provider and CDN may process standard technical information such as IP address, user agent, request time, and requested files in order to deliver the site and maintain operational security.

External links

The learning content may include links to third-party resources such as GitHub, technical references, and external learning material. Those destinations operate under their own privacy policies.
